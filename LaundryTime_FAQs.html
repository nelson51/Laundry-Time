FAQs
Q: How do I log out?
A: You must log out via the Profile Page.

Q: How do I report a broken machine?
A: Click on the machine that is broken on the Home Page, and then click 'Report'.

Q: How do I switch laundry rooms?
A: You must switch laundry room via the Profile Page.

Q: Why doesn't my live feed show up?
A: You laundry room either does not share its live feed with us or does not have a live feed at all.

Q: How can I contact Laundry Time?
A: You can contact us via the 'Contact Us' button at the top of this page.

Q: Who does gets contacted through the 'Contact Us' numbers?
A: The numbers on the Contact Us Page get directed to Laundry Time's support team. If there is an issue not involving the app, contact you should not contact our team.
